STORE_SALES -- ANALYTICS DASHBOARD


Situation:-  Vrinda Store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022, Hence Vrinda will be in a postion to understand the customer behaviour and Patterns to Anticpate the upcoming trends .

Task:- Acted as a  Data analyst to create a dashboard for an organisation.

Understanding requirements:-


*Compare the sales and order using single chart.

*Who purchased more- men or women in 2022

*Sates contribution in sales.

*Which size in the Product contributing to volume.

*Age group Contribution to sales.

*channel and month wise sales and order

Action:-

*Data Cleaning:-clean the irrelevant and unused data.

*Data transformation:-transform the data according to the needs in excel using formulas.

*Data Visualisation:-created interactive visuals by using pivottables and pivocharts.


Key Insights:-

1)Sales amount and orders fell steeply from the begining of the year to end .

2)Women contribution in sales is more comapred to men by  ~30% .

3) Sales among Adult,Senior,Teenage Adult contributes almost equivalent to senior and teenage combined together.
   
4) All the sizes exccept 4XL,5XL,6XL contributes less volume in sales.
   
5) Among states MH,KA,TN,UP Contributes sales more than 10%.
